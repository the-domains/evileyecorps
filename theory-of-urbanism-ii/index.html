Theory of Urbanism II

The goal of this course was to develop a more theoretical understanding of certain urban ideologies (in contrast to the usual practical exposure). Each student had to choose a text of interest (from a predefined list) and develop it. Programming the Urban Surface by Alex Wall was explicated in this case.
